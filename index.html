Meditation Countdown

Purpose of this app is for you to be able to keep focus while meditating. While meditating you will set a timer on this app and you will hear a stream of water random sounds of water drips throughout the countdown. You will need to keep count of the water drips. After the count down has ended you will imput the number of times you heard the water drip and see if you were able to keep count of all the water drips.
